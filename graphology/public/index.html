How Does Graphology Work??

Our handwriting is a depiction of our behaviour and mentality. How our body reacts in that way. When we write, our handwriting gets our body and mentally traits. Study and research has been done in handwriting traits which are related to our behaviours. Seeing those traits we can relate them with the users behaviour. Which generally come to be true.

Some of the traits in graphology...

The Slant

The slant trait of the handwriting show tell us about the one's affirmative or disallowance behaviour of the person.

The Base Line

The Baseline is the bottom line of our text. It tells us about the one's state. Straight baseline indicates confident and persistent behaviour . Falling base line indicates depression & suppression. Upward base line means Happy, Optimistic and Growing mindset of one's personality.

The Margin

The space from left side is called Margin, indicates the distance from our family. If the margin is less, the person is close to family. If the margin space is more then person is away from family. You can see spaced margin in Hostel students, or defence people who are away from family for a Long time.

Graphology Therapy

As we know that handwriting is released with behaviour and when behaviour changes, handwriting changes. Graphology therapy believes in changing in the handwriting to make some behavioural changes in the person. We can figure out the changes that we want in ourselves and try to change the handwriting as per the traits and we can reach to our goals.
